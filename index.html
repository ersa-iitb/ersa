Department of Earth Sciences

Overview

The Earth Sciences Association (ERSA) is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing knowledge and collaboration in geoscience disciplines including geology, geochemistry, geophysics and environmental science. ERSA promotes scientific curiosity through research discussions, academic events, field activities and collaborative projects.
